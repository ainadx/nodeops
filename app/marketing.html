Questions, answered

Do I need the hardware to try it?

No. The console runs against a mock fleet of six nodes in varied health states, so you can explore provisioning, bring-up, monitoring, and control with zero hardware.

Is it safe to control signals from a phone?

Control is confirmed, idempotent, time-bounded, and logged. Manual overrides auto-expire. Control is refused without a live link — and the node itself fails safe to all-red on watchdog or link loss, independent of the app and cloud.

One node or a hundred?

Both. A lightweight maker single-node mode and a city fleet view with a map, KPIs, alerts, RBAC, and an audit log — the same app, progressively disclosed.

Is it open source?

The hardware and firmware are open. NodeOps is self-hostable — bring your own cloud, broker, and SIM. The build assistant reads the same BOM and connection maps as the docs, so they never drift.
